Intro

I am developing a multiplayer format for FaB and an expansion that can be played with it. Both "Siege" and "Landmarks" are currently works in progress, and I am continuing to playtest and balance as needed.

If you are interested in downloading the assets and trying it out, join my Discord. All digital assets are free. I would love to hear any thoughts, questions, and/or feedback you may have!

Check out Siege and Landmarks.

Siege

Siege is an unoffical, fan-made format.

It is a casual, multiplayer format for FaB where friends come together and divide into teams of 2. The goal is to protect your castle and destroy the enemies'.

Siege resembles two-headed giant.

Deck Construction:

  • Siege currently follows Blitz Deck Construction
  • Young Heroes
  • 11 card Inventory (Weapons and Equipment)
  • 40 Card Deck
  • Up to 2 copies of each unique card

Card Legality

  • Siege follows UPF legality with the exception of special promo cards, adjudicator heroes, and Yorick, Weaver of Tales. So, almost anything goes…for now

Structure and Rules:

  • Base 2v2 PvPvE
  • First turn of the game rule applies normally (All players draw cards up to their hero's intellect at the end of the first turn)
  • Each team starts with a "Structure" and other supporting assets (This Structure is also referred to as a "Castle".)
  • Each Structure starts with 40 life
  • If your Structure's life goes to 0, your team loses
  • Players can block for their Structure, but not for their teammates
  • Players can “Respawn” (i.e., return to the battlefield after specified time in extended rules)

Extended rules: Siege Rulings

Start of Game Procedure:

  1. Players come to the table with a 40 card deck
  2. Players reveal their hero card
  3. Players choose teams (This is done at the discretion of the playgroup. I recommend this to be random)
  4. Players roll for turn:
    • Each player rolls a die
    • The player with the highest roll will choose to go either first or third. Their teammate's turn order will be the option they did not choose, either first or third
    • Turn order will always proceed in an "hourglass" fashion with whoever goes first followed by the enemy player directly in front of them (Teammates will always be next to each other and enemies across. Turns will alternate teams.)
  5. All players draw cards up to their hero's intellect and begin

Landmarks

Landmarks are an expansion to Siege, but can also be played in any format. They function as “World Effects” for the entirety of the game.

Before the start of the game–after teams are decided and heroes are revealed–players will shuffle the landmarks together and pick one randomly. The revealed card will be the active world effect, and players must engage in the mechanics specified on the card.

Players can think of themselves as battling in this area.

Rulings: Landmark Rulings

This is an ongoing project; I am continuing to work on and create unreleased Landmarks.
